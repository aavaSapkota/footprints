Welcome to Food Prints; a webapp for the everyday user looking to better understand their impact on the planet.

The Problem

People lack insight on the effects their behaviors have on the world.

In 2017, Canadians were reported to have emitted 15.59 tons per capita of CO2 emissions. This is one of the highest in the world.

How can we lower this number?

Receipts

Here at Food Prints, we provide a quick and easy way for you to check your climate footprint on a day to day basis. By simply uploading pictures of your receipts, we relay to you valuable insights about your purchased items and how much CO2 was emitted to get them to you.
